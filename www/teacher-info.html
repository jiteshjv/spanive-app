TYPES OF TEACHERS
PT
Personal Teachers
Personal Teachers are certified language instructors who provide customized one-on-one lessons tailored to your specific needs and learning pace.
  • Personalized learning experience
  • Focused on your specific goals
  • Regular progress tracking
  • Flexible scheduling options
GT
General Teachers
General Teachers provide structured lessons following a curriculum designed to improve your overall language skills through guided practice in a group setting.
  • Structured learning path
  • Diverse conversation topics
  • Interact with other learners
  • Always available
